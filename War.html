War


  • It is played between 20 players.
  • If you die in this match , you will join the match again.
  • Play area is small.
  • Can be played in both FPP and TPP.
  • Can be played in solo,duo and squad.
  • Maps are selected randomly.
  • Every player has same appearance.
  • Battleground zone does not decrease.
  • Players drop in the battleground with wepons.
  • The squad, duo or the player having the hghest rank in the game wins the war.
  • War mode is not ranked
