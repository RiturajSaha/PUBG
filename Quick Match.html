Quick Match


  • 28 to 30 players join in this game.
  • Play area is small.
  • Can be played in both FPP and TPP.
  • Can be played in solo,duo and squad.
  • Duration of this mode is 8 minutes.
  • Maps are selected randomly.
  • Type of match can be shotguns only, melee only, pistols only, iem heaven etc.
  • Battleground zone decreases.
  • Quick match mode is not ranked.
