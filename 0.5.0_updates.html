MAP

Miramaris the 2nd playable map for BATTLEGROUNDS. Initially slated to be a 4x4 km map,Miramar is a 8x8km city-centric map with a open desert plain and rural areas.Development on this map began in July 2017, PUBG Corp used the official release update (1.0 update) to test Miramar in 3 rounds of testing phases.


ROYALE PASS SEASON 2
  1. A brand new feature!
  2. Now you can collect rewards for every new tier reached in Season 2.
  3. Complete daily and weekly missions to earn points and increase your rank. Complete a certain number of missions every week for additional crates.
  4. Reach a new Royale Pass rank to unlock its rewards. Players with a busy schedule have the option to purchase ranks directly to unlock rewards.
  5. An elite version of the Royale Pass is also available. Purchasing the Elite Royale Pass unlocks elite missions to earn more points and collect additional rewards for each rank.
