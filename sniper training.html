Sniper Training


  • Almost 50 players join in this game.
  • Play area is small.
  • Can be played in both FPP and TPP.
  • Can be played in solo,duo and squad.
  • Duration of this mode is 15 minutes.
  • Maps are selected randomly.
  • Mostly long range guns are used.
  • Battleground zone decreases.
  • Sniper Training mode is not ranked.
